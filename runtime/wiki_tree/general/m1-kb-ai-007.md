> author: m1 · confidence: 0.85


From the harness framework:

| Criterion | What It Measures |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Design Quality** | Coherent whole vs collection of parts; colors, typography, layout, imagery create distinct mood |
| **Originality** | Custom decisions vs template defaults vs AI-generated patterns (purple gradients = fail) |
| **Craft** | Technical execution: typography hierarchy, spacing, color harmony, contrast — competence not creativity |
