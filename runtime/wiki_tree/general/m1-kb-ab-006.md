> author: m1 · confidence: 0.85

- 2D游戏制作工具：关卡编辑器(tile-based, multi-layer support)
- 2D游戏制作工具：精灵编辑器(pixel drawing, frame animation)
- 2D游戏制作工具：实体行为系统(Patrol/Chase/Gravity/Collectible)

- 像素渲染：imageSmoothingEnabled=false + nearest-neighbor缩放
